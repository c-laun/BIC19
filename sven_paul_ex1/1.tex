\section*{Exercise 1}
As there are approximately $10^{11}$ neurons in the brain, each firing at around
1 Hz, and the total energy use is 20 W, we calculate the energy use per neuron:
\begin{align}
    E_{spike} &= \frac{20 J/s}{10^{11}*1/s}\\
    &= 2 * 10^{-10} J
\end{align}
